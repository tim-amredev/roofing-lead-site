Understanding Roofing Costs

What Affects Roofing Costs?

  • Roof Size: Larger roofs require more materials and labor.
  • Roof Pitch: Steeper roofs are more difficult and dangerous to work on.
  • Material Quality: Higher quality materials last longer but cost more.
  • Complexity: Multiple levels, dormers, and skylights increase cost.
  • Location: Labor and material costs vary by region.
